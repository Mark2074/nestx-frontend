NestX — Creator Terms & Monetization

Creator Terms & Monetization

Version: 1.1 — Last update: 2026-03-06

1) Scope

These Creator Terms apply when you request and/or use Creator features on NestX, including hosting events/live sessions and requesting payouts. By submitting a Creator request, you confirm you have read and accepted these terms.

2) Platform approval (Creator access)

Creator access is not automatic. You must request access and NestX may approve or reject the request at its sole discretion to protect the platform and users.

  • NestX may request additional information to assess authenticity and safety.
  • NestX may suspend or revoke Creator access at any time for risk, safety, or policy reasons.

3) Age and legality

You confirm you are at least 18 years old and that all content you publish on NestX is legal in your jurisdiction and in the jurisdictions where you choose to make it available.

4) Your responsibilities

  • You are solely responsible for your content, conduct, and communications.
  • You must not upload illegal content, exploitative content, or content involving minors (zero tolerance).
  • You must not impersonate others or misrepresent identity, age, or ownership of content.
  • You must respect user privacy and platform safety rules.

5) Stripe-first compliance (payouts)

Payouts and payout compliance are handled through Stripe. NestX does not collect or store your identity documents for payout compliance. To receive payouts, you must complete Stripe onboarding and any verification steps required by Stripe.

  • NestX may require a valid Stripe payout account to enable payouts.
  • If Stripe limits or disables payouts, NestX payouts are not available until Stripe restores eligibility.

6) Tokens, earnings, and payout holds

Your earnings on NestX may be subject to risk holds for fraud prevention, disputes, and platform safety (including abusive behavior or policy violations).

  • Token earnings may be delayed before becoming withdrawable.
  • NestX may freeze or withhold payouts during investigations.

7) Chargebacks, disputes, fraud, and clawbacks

If a payment used to purchase tokens is later disputed, reversed, charged back, or identified as fraud, NestX may reverse related token earnings and/or deduct balances as needed to cover losses and platform costs.

  • This includes cases where tokens were transferred, tipped, or used to buy access to your events.
  • NestX may pause payouts until disputes are resolved.

8) Anti-fraud and AML cooperation

You agree not to engage in fraud, manipulation, laundering, or abusive monetization patterns, including self-tipping schemes, collusion, bot activity, or artificial inflation of earnings.

You agree to cooperate with NestX and Stripe in reasonable investigations related to fraud, AML concerns, and compliance requests.

9) Intellectual Property, Live Content, and Broadcast Responsibility

You must only publish, upload, stream, broadcast, or otherwise make available content that you own or have the legal right to use. You must not upload stolen, pirated, unauthorized, or infringing content.

You are fully responsible for all content transmitted during live sessions, events, private sessions, uploaded media, and any other creator activity on NestX.

This includes any music, background audio, video, television broadcasts, radio streams, sports events, films, clips, or other third-party material that may appear in or be heard during your live sessions or content.

You must ensure that you have all necessary rights, permissions, authorizations, and licenses required to use such material. NestX does not obtain, provide, or pay for copyright licenses, public performance licenses, or similar rights on behalf of creators.

Creators are strongly advised not to play background music, radio, television broadcasts, or any other copyrighted audio during live sessions unless they personally hold the necessary rights or licenses.

NestX may remove content, interrupt live sessions, restrict creator features, suspend payouts, or suspend accounts where infringement or unauthorized use of third-party content is suspected or reported.

10) Termination and enforcement

NestX may suspend, restrict, or terminate Creator access and/or your account if needed for platform safety, policy enforcement, legal compliance, or risk management.

11) Changes to these terms

NestX may update these Creator Terms. When you submit a Creator request, you accept the current version shown on this page and referenced in your request.

Practical note
Creator approval (platform-level) and Stripe payout verification are separate steps. First NestX approves Creator access. Later, when you request payouts, Stripe onboarding/verification applies.
