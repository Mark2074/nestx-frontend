Introduction

Tokens on NestX are internal platform credits. They exist to unlock features, support creators, and maintain a sustainable ecosystem.

Tokens are not money, not a currency, and not an investment product. NestX does not promise earnings, financial returns, or economic results.

3.1 What Tokens Are

  • Digital credits usable only within NestX.
  • A tool for access, interaction, and creator support.
  • A system designed to reduce spam and abusive behavior by attaching value to certain actions.

In short: tokens are a platform utility, not a financial instrument.

3.2 How Tokens Are Used

Tokens may be used for features such as:

  • Chat and interactive actions where access rules apply.
  • Live rooms and CAM-related interactions.
  • Event access and ticketing (where applicable).
  • Internal promotion and platform visibility tools.
  • Premium features and enhanced functionalities, when available.
Important
All token usage is subject to platform rules, moderation, and anti-abuse safeguards.

3.3 Monetization Rules

Not all users can monetize on NestX.

  • Users may purchase and spend tokens within the platform.
  • Approved Creators may receive tokens through official platform flows.
  • Token redemption (payout) is available only to eligible, approved Creators.

Tokens received are not automatically equivalent to money. Redemption is a separate process and may require additional compliance steps.

NestX reserves the right to limit or suspend monetization in cases of fraud, abuse, disputes, or policy violations.

3.4 What Tokens Are Not

  • Not an official currency.
  • Not an investment.
  • Not a financial product.
  • Not a guarantee of income or visibility.

NestX does not guarantee audience growth, visibility, earnings, or monetization success.

Security & Integrity
To protect users and the platform, certain transactions may be reviewed. In cases of suspected fraud or disputes, related token operations may be limited or restricted.
