Introduction

The ADV system on NestX is an internal promotion tool dedicated to approved Creators. It is designed to increase visibility of events and compliant content within the platform ecosystem.

ADV is not external advertising and not an open advertising marketplace. It is a structured internal visibility tool.

5.1 Internal ADV

ADV can be created by approved Creators.

Each Creator has a limited number of free ADV placements per day (currently 2). Additional promotions may require token usage according to active platform rules.

ADV may promote:

  • Events
  • Live sessions
  • Content compliant with platform rules

Promotion does not guarantee audience, results, or earnings. Distribution follows internal platform logic.

5.2 Moderation & Control

All ADV content must comply with:

  • Platform content rules
  • Anti-spam policies
  • Authenticity and coherence principles

NestX may, at any time:

  • Limit the distribution of an ADV
  • Remove non-compliant ADV content
  • Suspend access to the ADV function in case of abuse

An ADV may be removed after publication if it violates platform rules.

5.3 Difference Between ADV and Posts

A Post is organic content published in the feed.

An ADV is content promoted through a structured internal visibility tool.

  • Posts rely on organic distribution.
  • ADV uses a regulated internal promotion system.

ADV enhances visibility but does not replace content quality and does not guarantee success.

Final Principle
The ADV system exists to support sustainable growth. Manipulative, deceptive, or abusive use may result in loss of access to the feature.
