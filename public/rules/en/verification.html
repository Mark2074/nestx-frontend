Introduction

Verification on NestX exists to confirm that a profile represents a real person. It is designed to reduce impersonation and fake accounts, and to increase transparency between users.

Verification is not mandatory, but it strengthens trust within the platform.

7.1 Verification Procedure

Verification consists of submitting a short video according to platform instructions.

The video must clearly show:

  • The account holder (the face may be masked or non-recognizable).
  • A clearly visible sheet of paper containing:
    • Username
    • Date
    • The word “NestX”

The submitted material is reviewed by the platform.

If approved:

  • The account is marked as verified.
  • The verification video is published on the user’s profile page.
  • The video is publicly visible as proof of authenticity.

Publication occurs only after platform approval. If the material is not compliant, the verification request may be rejected.

7.2 Authenticity & Coherence

A verified profile must maintain coherence between:

  • The declared identity.
  • The person shown in the verification video.
  • The content published on the account.

Verification may be revoked in cases of:

  • Impersonation.
  • Use of non-authentic material.
  • Violation of platform rules.

7.3 What Verification Does Not Guarantee

  • Permanent compliant behavior.
  • Absence of future rule violations.
  • Automatic approval of all content.

Verification confirms profile identity, not user reputation.

Principle
Authenticity is an ongoing responsibility. Verification may be revoked if platform rules are violated.
