Introduction

NestX adopts a moderation system to maintain a safe, coherent, and respectful environment.

Moderation is an essential tool to protect the platform and its community.

8.1 Automated Moderation

NestX uses automated systems to detect potentially problematic content.

  • Flag suspicious content
  • Temporarily limit visibility
  • Send content for review

Final decisions are made by the platform after evaluation.

8.2 Human Moderation

The platform may intervene through manual review.

  • Remove content
  • Restrict account features
  • Revoke privileges
  • Suspend or terminate accounts

Actions may be taken at any time to ensure safety, compliance, and platform integrity.

8.3 Reporting System

Users may report content or behavior they consider problematic.

  • Reports are reviewed by the platform
  • Reports do not automatically trigger immediate action

Abuse of the reporting system may result in limitations.

8.4 Safety Measures

To protect the ecosystem, NestX may:

  • Temporarily restrict features
  • Block suspicious access
  • Limit monetization-related functions during verification processes

Measures may be preventive or reactive.

Shared Responsibility
Safety is a shared responsibility. The platform may intervene when necessary according to its internal rules and policies.
