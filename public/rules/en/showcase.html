Introduction

The Showcase is an exhibition space reserved for users with an active VIP status. It exists to present personal creations, projects, and work made by users.

The Showcase is not a commercial system and is not intended for selling products or services.

6.1 What the Showcase is

The Showcase is a structured section where a VIP user can display a personal creation in a clean and organized way.

Examples may include:

  • Handmade creations
  • Personal projects
  • Original work made by the user
  • Concrete, real creations and hobby work

The purpose of the Showcase is exhibition, not commerce.

6.2 Access and approval

Publishing in the Showcase:

  • Is reserved for VIP users.
  • Requires prior approval by the platform.
  • May be limited in the number of active items.

The platform reviews submissions to ensure coherence, quality, and compliance with section standards. A submission may be rejected or removed at any time if it does not meet the Showcase requirements.

6.3 Section limits

  • The Showcase is not a marketplace.
  • The Showcase is not a sales tool.
  • The Showcase must not be used to promote transactions.
  • The Showcase must not be used to organize deals or commercial agreements.

Any attempt to use the Showcase for selling or external commercial arrangements is against the spirit and rules of the platform.

Important
Personal initiatives, if they happen, are the sole responsibility of the users involved and are not encouraged or facilitated by the platform.

6.4 Moderation & control

Showcase content must:

  • Be authentic and original.
  • Be coherent with the user profile and description.
  • Respect platform content rules.
  • Not promote illegal or dangerous activity.

NestX may remove non-compliant content, revoke access to the section, or limit usage in case of abuse.

Final note
The Showcase exists to highlight personal creations and projects — not to enable commercial activity.
