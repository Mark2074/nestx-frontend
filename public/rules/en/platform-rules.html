NestX — Platform Rules

Platform Rules (Core Rules)

Version: 1.1 — Last update: 2026-03-06
Rules Home Account Types Moderation Terms

NestX is built for freedom of expression — but freedom only works within clear boundaries. These rules exist to protect users, protect the platform, and keep the ecosystem sustainable over time.

Simple idea
You can express yourself, but you must respect people, consent, authenticity, and the law. When in doubt: choose the safer option.

1.1 Respect & Conduct

Treat others as real people. You can disagree, you can be direct, you can be ironic — but you cannot harass, threaten, dehumanize, or target people with hate.

Allowed: criticism Allowed: strong opinions Not allowed: harassment Not allowed: hate / dehumanization
  • No threats, doxxing, intimidation, or coordinated harassment.
  • No hate speech or targeted abuse based on personal attributes.
  • No persistent unwanted contact or pressure.

1.2 Adult Content Rules

NestX can include adult content, but it must stay within clear boundaries: consensual, legal, adult-only, and not exploitative. The platform is not a place for illegal or coercive material.

Allowed: consensual adult content Not allowed: minors (zero tolerance) Not allowed: coercion / non-consensual
  • All participants must be 18+ and consenting.
  • No content involving minors, or content that even suggests minors.
  • No non-consensual content, revenge material, hidden recordings, or exploitation.
Grey areas (conceptual)
If a piece of content could reasonably be interpreted as non-consensual, coercive, or unsafe, it may be removed without extended debate. Safety wins.

1.3 Consent & Authenticity

Consent is not just a word. It means the content is shared with permission, participants agree, and users are not misled about the nature of what they are seeing.

Allowed: authentic presence Risk: misleading identity Not allowed: impersonation
  • No impersonation or identity misrepresentation that harms others.
  • No stolen content presented as your own.
  • Profile/content should be coherent enough to avoid misleading users.

1.4 Spam, Abuse & Manipulation

NestX is not a growth-hack playground. Spam, automation abuse, and manipulation reduce trust and damage discovery.

Allowed: normal promotion Not allowed: bots / spam Not allowed: system bypass
  • No bot activity, fake engagement, or automated abuse.
  • No attempts to bypass limits, paywalls, moderation, or restrictions.
  • No mass unsolicited messages or “follow me” flooding patterns.

1.5 Illegal Content (Zero tolerance)

Some categories are non-negotiable. If content falls into illegal or high-risk territory, enforcement is strict and immediate.

Zero tolerance: minors Zero tolerance: illegal activity Zero tolerance: violent illegal content
  • Any content involving minors is forbidden. No exceptions.
  • No promotion or facilitation of illegal activity.
  • Enforcement may include content removal, restrictions, and account actions.

1.6 Copyright & Third-Party Content

Respect the work of others. Content shared on NestX must not violate copyright or other third-party rights.

Allowed: content you own Allowed: content you have permission to use Not allowed: pirated media Not allowed: unauthorized broadcasts
  • No streaming of movies, TV channels, sports events, or radio broadcasts without rights.
  • No playing copyrighted music during live sessions unless you have the legal right to use it.
  • No uploading stolen or unauthorized media.
Reminder
NestX does not provide licenses for music, broadcasts, or copyrighted media. If you stream or upload third-party content, you must have the right to do so.
