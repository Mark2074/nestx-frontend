Account Types & Status

On NestX there are different account types and statuses. Each level enables specific features and has defined limits, designed to ensure security, authenticity, and sustainability for both users and the platform.

Your account type defines what you can do. Your status may extend or enhance certain capabilities.

2.1 Base User

What a Base User can do

  • Register and create a profile.
  • Publish posts in the feed in compliance with platform rules.
  • View public content.
  • Follow other users and interact with content.
  • Access the main sections of the platform.
  • Access live rooms and events according to applicable access rules.
  • Send messages within defined limits.
  • Purchase tokens (when enabled).

What a Base User cannot do

  • Monetize content or events.
  • Redeem tokens.
  • Access Creator-only privileges.

2.2 Verified User

What verification is

Verification is a procedure designed to confirm the authenticity of a profile. It is completed through a verification video following the methods specified by the platform.

  • Confirms that the profile represents a real person.
  • Increases trust within the community.
  • Improves credibility in interactions.

Verification confirms authenticity, not reputation.

What verification does not do

  • It does not enable monetization by itself.
  • It does not replace VIP status.
  • It does not grant Creator privileges.

2.3 VIP Status

VIP is an additional status, not a separate account type. It may be activated according to platform rules.

VIP provides enhanced platform features and extended usage limits compared to standard accounts.

  • Does not convert a user into a Creator.
  • Does not enable monetization by itself.
  • Does not guarantee visibility or earnings.
  • Does not override moderation rules or legal requirements.

VIP benefits remain active only while the status is valid. When VIP expires, associated enhancements are automatically removed.

2.4 Creator Account

Requirements

  • Platform approval (Creator access is not automatic).
  • Acceptance of Creator-specific terms and conditions.
  • Full compliance with platform content rules.

Rights

  • Create and manage live sessions and events.
  • Receive tokens through official platform features.
  • Promote content within permitted limits.
  • Access Creator tools where available.

Responsibilities

  • Publish only compliant content.
  • Respect users and the community.
  • Avoid misleading or manipulative behavior.
  • Properly manage live sessions and events.
  • Accept platform reviews and compliance checks.

Allowed monetization

  • Monetization is permitted exclusively through official platform token systems and flows.
  • External or unauthorized monetization methods are not allowed.
  • Token redemption is available only to approved Creators who complete required payout onboarding.
General principle
NestX is built on a balance between freedom of expression and clear rules. Misuse of platform features, regardless of account type or status, may result in limitations, suspensions, or revocation of privileges.
